About Opidy

Opidy (OPportunities for your Interests DirectorY) is an opportunity-oriented network that helps users know and share opportunities that match their interests and meet their needs. The network provides its users with interest-related advisers that give them insights as they explore on ways to grow their interest and solve their unique needs.

We pride ourselves as the place where you can “know opportunities on just anything!”

The mission of Opidy stems from the concept that social interaction should be about what is important to people; their interest; their needs and the opportunities they seek.

Our unique algorithm of interaction ensures you only focus on what matters to you as your interaction is based on your interest not on Opiders.

How it works

Focused
Interaction

John and Alice choose to peer as they have Business as a common interest. The mutual interest is the only possibility for their peering.

Sharing

An opportunity shared by that is not common to Alice will simply not give her a notification to keep a focus on Alices' interest.

Tagging

When Alice shares a content or opportunity that is not owned by her she tags the owner with an author tag to indicate the source.
